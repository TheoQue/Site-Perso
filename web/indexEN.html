My sporting career

I started canoeing 10 years ago at Pays de Brocéliande club. I joined Pôle Espoir in the same time as high school. This dual cursus offered me a chance to strongly progress while continuing my studies.
With a 10 hours weekly training program, I finished my under 18 season with a 5th place at 2014 nationnal selections. I'm already competing in second nationnal divison as a senior and hope to improve my level until upgrading in the first division.
I'm still training and coaching young athletes as well as studying. Loving my origin club, I'm also enjoying camps and trainings with younger people.
